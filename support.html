Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save my progress?

GeoBurst automatically saves your progress locally on your device. Meta upgrades, unlocked cores, and fragment balance persist between runs.

How do I remove ads?

You can purchase "Remove Ads" from the main menu to permanently disable interstitial and banner advertisements. Rewarded ads (optional, for bonuses) remain available if you choose to use them.

I lost my progress after reinstalling. Can I recover it?

Currently, game data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app will remove all saved data. Cloud save is planned for a future update.

The game is running slowly. What can I do?

Go to the settings menu and try lowering the quality preset. GeoBurst supports Low, Medium, and High quality settings to accommodate different devices.

How do Daily Challenges work?

A new challenge appears every day with unique modifiers (enemy buffs, weapon restrictions, etc.). Everyone gets the same challenge each day. Complete it to earn bonus fragments!
