GeoBurst - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 20, 2026

Bartug Kaan Celebi ("we", "our", or "us") built GeoBurst as a free-to-play mobile game. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

GeoBurst does not require account creation, login, or any personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

On iOS 14.5 and later, GeoBurst requests your permission before allowing AdMob to track your activity across other apps and websites. You can change this permission at any time in your device Settings under Privacy > Tracking.

If you decline tracking, you will still see ads, but they will be less personalized.

Local Data Storage

GeoBurst stores game progress, settings, and preferences locally on your device using Unity's PlayerPrefs system. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to any server
  • Is not shared with any third party
  • Can be deleted by uninstalling the app

Children's Privacy

GeoBurst is rated 13+ and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

Ad-Free Option

GeoBurst offers a "Remove Ads" in-app purchase that permanently removes interstitial and banner advertisements. When ads are removed, AdMob's data collection related to ad serving is also disabled.

Data Security

We do not operate servers or databases for GeoBurst. All game data is stored locally on your device. Third-party data collection by AdMob is governed by Google's security practices.
